About FD

F Designs is also an accomplished painter and sculptor. The F Designs Art Gallery at Atria Mall, Worli showcases his work which has been executed in a variety of mediums from oil to acrylic. For the propagation of his unique thinking and approach to automotive design F Designs has partnered with the D Y Patil Group to start DYPF Designs Center for Automotive Research and Studies which will nurture the next generation of Indian automotive designers. Behind every successful business is a person whose passion and vision drives every facet of the organization.
F Designs has become the authority in automotive design in India by single mindedly following his passion.F Designs

our VALUES

It is said that if you love what you do for a living then you will never see it as ‘just a job’. By that maxim Dilip Chhabria has possibly never ever worked a day in his life. For him the business of car design and modification is an amalgamation of his passion, hobby and work. In F Designs we see the marriage of an artist’s sensibilities and an engineer’s mind. He infuses each of his projects with an aesthetic awareness while simultaneously pushing the machine’s capabilities to a new level. Every F Designs creation is a signature extension of the artist’s personality and while never two of his products are the same, they are instantly recognizable as having made by the same master.

CONCEPTS

The cutting-edge of automotive design. Created every two years, F DesignsD’s concept cars redefine the status-quo. These are limited edition collections priced by a few who won’t settle for just ultra-premium.

STANDARD

This is what every car wants to grow up to be. F DesignsD has custom interior and exterior variations available for Toyota Innova & Fortuner. There are also exterior body kits for Swift, i20, City and the Tata Nano. These modifications include luxury interiors, attractive exteriors and a whole host of technology options. That little F Designs logo on your car will mean that you are a part of an exclusive club that appreciates refined taste and do not settle for ordinary.

SPECIALITY

A unique, one-of-a-kind creation. A F DesignsD masterpiece. A commitment to perfection in the chosen field. Only masters of artistry and craftsmanship can cater to such demanding requirements and fit them in a vehicle without compromising it. For example, creating a mobile office that caters to a company which likes to work on the move!

CUSTOM

Conceptualized and created to your exact specifications. An extension of your personality. Work closely with the F DesignsD team to create a unique automotive that has been crafted especially for you, like a bespoke Italian suit.

INTERIORS

For those few whose luxury lives and breathes with them, F DesignsD’s interior design brings sophistication and opulence to your home. Each creation is equipped with the latest technology and is the final word in comfort.
